About Tatiana Perry

Hello, my name is Tatiana Perry. I am a graduate from Indiana University Purdue University (IUPUI) getting my Bachelors of Science Degree in Media Arts & Science specializing in Web Design & Development. I also have an Associates Degree in Visual Communications specializing in Graphics Design with a minor in Photography. I love all aspecs of digital design. Most of my work in my porfolio today are of pieces that I have worked on in school and transformed them into different context. I love what I am able to do with all my degrees. I am a very outgoing individual with a lot of things to learn still, but i absolutly love the journey so far.
